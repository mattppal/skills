## AIRPORT & FLIGHT INFO

### Kahului Airport (OGG)
- Small airport, easy to navigate
- Security usually quick (but arrive 1.5-2 hrs early for inter-island or mainland)
- Food options inside security
- Free wifi

### Rental Car Return
- Follow signs for rental car return
- Fill gas if required by rental agreement
- Check car for all your belongings
- Shuttle to terminal usually quick

### TSA Notes
- Liquids must be 3.4 oz or less
- Any reef-safe sunscreen in checked bag
- No fresh fruit to mainland (agriculture restrictions)
- Lei can be brought if properly packaged
