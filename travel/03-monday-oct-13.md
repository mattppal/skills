# MONDAY, OCT 13 - ROAD TO HANA

## TODAY'S VIBE
The legendary Road to Hana. This is what people come to Maui for. 60+ miles of the world's most scenic coastal highway with 620 curves, 59 bridges, waterfalls around every corner, and ending with an epic hike through a bamboo forest to a 400-foot waterfall.

**Total Driving:** 4+ hours (2+ hours each way)  
**This is a SCENIC DRIVE - the drive IS the activity**  
**Difficulty:** Moderate driving, moderate hiking  
**Energy Level:** High - long day, early start, lots of adventure  
**Return Time:** 6-7pm to Kihei

### THE EPIC HIKE

**12:45pm-3:00pm - PIPIWAI TRAIL TO WAIMOKU FALLS**
- **Distance:** 3.4 miles round trip (some sources say 4 miles)
- **Time:** 2-2.5 hours total
- **Difficulty:** Moderate (some elevation, muddy sections)
- **Rating:** 4.8/5 (9,214 reviews - #1 rated hike on Maui)
- **AllTrails:** https://www.alltrails.com/trail/hawaii/maui/pipiwai-trail
- **Also search:** "Waimoku Falls via Pipiwai Trail" on AllTrails
- **Official Site:** Haleakala National Park - Kipahulu District

**What You'll See:**
1. **Seven Sacred Pools ('Ohe'o Gulch)** - First section
   - Series of pools and waterfalls
   - Can swim if open (check with rangers)
   - Bridge crossings

2. **Makahiku Falls** - ~0.5 miles in
   - 200-foot waterfall viewpoint
   - Quick side trail

3. **BAMBOO FOREST** - ~1.2 miles in
   - This is the iconic section
   - Walk through towering bamboo
   - Magical, otherworldly
   - Amazing for photos
   - Can be muddy

4. **WAIMOKU FALLS** - End of trail
   - 400-foot waterfall (MASSIVE)
   - The payoff
   - Spray from falls is refreshing
   - Don't get too close to base (rockfall danger)

**Trail Tips:**
- Wear hiking shoes (trail can be muddy, slippery)
- Bring trekking poles if you have them
- Take your time in bamboo forest (photo ops)
- Stay on marked trail
- Bug spray can be helpful

**3:00pm - Explore Seven Sacred Pools**
- If open for swimming, take a dip
- Otherwise, just admire the pools
- Use facilities before driving back

**3:15pm - Optional: Waioka Pond (Venus Pool)**

## DRIVING TIPS FOR ROAD TO HANA

### The Road Itself
- 620 curves, 59 bridges (most single-lane)
- SLOW DOWN - speed limit is 25 mph most places
- Pull over at wide spots to let faster drivers pass
- Single-lane bridges: Yield to oncoming traffic already on bridge
- It's not a race - enjoy the drive

### Parking
- Many stops have limited parking
- Pull fully off road
- Don't block driveways or local access
- Be respectful - locals live here

### Photos
- Don't take photos while driving
- Pull over at designated lookouts
- Passenger can take photos

### Etiquette
- Yield to locals (they drive this daily for work)
- Don't stop in middle of road
- Keep bathroom stops quick at facilities
- Pack out all trash
