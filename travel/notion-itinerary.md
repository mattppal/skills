### Trail Info
- **Find on AllTrails:** Search "Sliding Sands Haleakala" (ignore the 18-mile full trail option!)
- **You're hiking:** 2-4 miles total (1-2 miles in, then back out before sunset)
- **NOT the full 18-mile trail** (that's for overnight backpackers)
- **Official Info:** nps.gov/hale/planyourvisit/hiking.htm
- **Temp:** 30-40°F at summit (BRING WARM CLOTHES)
