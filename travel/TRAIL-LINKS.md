# MAUI HIKING TRAILS - WORKING LINKS & RESOURCES

## YOUR 3 MAIN HIKES

### 1. SUNDAY - Waihee Ridge Trail

**How to Find on AllTrails:**
- Search: "Waihee Ridge Trail" on AllTrails.com
- Or search: "Waihee Ridge Maui"
- Location: Wailuku, Maui

**Trail Stats:**
- Distance: 4.2 miles round trip
- Time: 2.5-3 hours
- Difficulty: Moderate-Hard
- Elevation gain: 1,500 ft
- Views: Panoramic valley and ocean

**Official Resources:**
- AllTrails general search: https://www.alltrails.com/hawaii/maui
- Then filter for "Waihee Ridge"

---

### 2. MONDAY - Pipiwai Trail to Waimoku Falls

**How to Find on AllTrails:**
- Search: "Pipiwai Trail" on AllTrails.com
- Or search: "Waimoku Falls Maui"
- Location: Kipahulu District, Haleakala National Park

**Trail Stats:**
- Distance: 3.4-4 miles round trip
- Time: 2-2.5 hours
- Difficulty: Moderate
- Highlights: Bamboo forest, Seven Sacred Pools, 400-foot waterfall

**Official Resources:**
- AllTrails general search: https://www.alltrails.com/hawaii/maui
- Then filter for "Pipiwai" or "Waimoku"
- **Official NPS:** https://www.nps.gov/hale/planyourvisit/kipahulu.htm

---

### 3. WEDNESDAY - Haleakala Crater Trails

**Sliding Sands Trail (Keoneheʻeheʻe Trail):**

**How to Find on AllTrails:**
- Search: "Sliding Sands Haleakala" on AllTrails.com
- Or search: "Keoneheehee Trail"
- Or just browse Haleakala trails on AllTrails
- Location: Haleakala Summit

**IMPORTANT - You're NOT Doing the Full 18-Mile Trail:**

**The Full Trail (What you'll see on AllTrails):**
- 18 miles round trip to Paliku Cabin
- All-day or overnight backpacking trip
- NOT what you're doing!

**What YOU'RE Actually Hiking:**
- **2-4 miles TOTAL** (1-2 miles into crater, then turn around)
- **1.5-2 hours max** (you only have ~2 hours before sunset)
- **Just go until views are great, then return for sunset**

**Realistic Options for Sunset Timing:**
- 1 mile in/out = 2 miles total (45 mins-1 hour)
- 1.5 miles in/out = 3 miles total (1.5 hours)
- 2 miles in/out = 4 miles total (2 hours max)

**Trail Stats:**
- Starts at 10,000 ft elevation
- Downhill going in (easy)
- Uphill coming out (harder at altitude)
- Highlights: Volcanic crater, otherworldly landscape, colorful cinder

**Official Resources:**
- **NPS Haleakala Official Site:** https://www.nps.gov/hale/planyourvisit/hiking.htm
- AllTrails Haleakala trails: https://www.alltrails.com/parks/us/hawaii/haleakala-national-park
- Search on AllTrails for: "Sliding Sands" or browse Haleakala park trails

**Alternative Trail at Haleakala:**
- **Halemau'u Trail** - Search "Halemau'u Haleakala" on AllTrails
- Different crater views, greener landscape
