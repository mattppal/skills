### AFTERNOON - WAILEA EXPLORATION

**2:00-5:00pm - The Shops at Wailea + Beach Walk**

**The Shops at Wailea**
- Address: 3750 Wailea Alanui Dr, Wailea
- Phone: (808) 891-6770
- Website: theshopsatwailea.com
- Hours: 10am-9pm daily
- Rating: 4.5/5
- Distance from Kihei: 8 mins

**What It Is:**
- Beautiful 2-level open-air luxury shopping mall
- Mix of high-end and standard shops
- Art galleries, tiki wood carvings, local artists
- Central courtyard with stage (sometimes hula shows/live performances on Saturdays!)
- Free parking

**Shops to Explore:**

**Upscale (Upper Level):**
- Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Prada, Saint Laurent (if you want to window shop luxury)
- Tiffany & Co. (just to admire the sparkle)

**More Accessible (Ground Level):**
- **Honolulu Cookie Company** - Famous Hawaiian cookies, great gifts
- **Soha Living** - Cool home decor, island style
- **Tommy Bahama** - Island clothing (also has restaurant/bar)
- **Martin & MacArthur** - Koa wood products, local crafts
- **Tiki carving store** - World-famous, incredible art pieces
- **Mahina** - Local boutique (also has Kihei location)
- **Art galleries** - Multiple, browse local Hawaiian art

**Food/Drink Options:**
- **Lappert's Hawaii** - Ice cream, sorbet (highly recommended)
- **Island Gourmet Markets** - Upscale grocery, prepared foods
- **Waikiki Brewing Company** - Pina coladas, Mai Tais, casual food
- **The Pint & Cork** - Bar with good beer selection

**Your Wailea Afternoon Route:**

**2:00pm:** Arrive at The Shops at Wailea, park in free lot

**2:00-3:30pm:** Shop + Explore
- Browse shops (mix of window shopping luxury + actual shopping)
- Check out tiki wood carving artist
- Art galleries
- Get ice cream at Lappert's
- See if there's live performance in courtyard (Saturdays often have hula shows)

**3:30pm:** Walk to Wailea Beach Path (right next to the shops)

**3:45-4:45pm:** WAILEA BEACH PATH WALK
- **What:** 1.5-mile coastal walking path connecting 5 Wailea resort beaches
- **Rating:** 4.8/5
- **Why it's special:** Paved path along stunning coastline, tide pools, lava rock, resort beaches
- **Beaches you'll pass:**
  - Ulua Beach
  - Wailea Beach
  - Polo Beach
  - Access to multiple resort areas
- **Activities:** Walk, explore beaches, tide pools, photos
- **Time:** 1-2 hours for full walk (or just do part of it)
- **Vibe:** Beautiful, well-maintained, mix of resort guests and locals

**4:45pm:** Return to car at The Shops

**5:00pm:** Drive back to Kihei (8 mins)

**Why This Works:**
- Preview the Wailea area you'll go through tomorrow on beach hopping
- Mix of shopping + outdoor coastal exploration
- Beautiful resort area to see
- Only 8 mins from Kihei, very efficient
