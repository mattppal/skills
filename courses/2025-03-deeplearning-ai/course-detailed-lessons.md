## Course delivery method

Each lesson combines:
- Guided instruction with clear explanations
- Hands-on coding with AI assistance
- Real-time problem-solving
- Practical application development

You'll complete two fully functional web applications by the end of the course, with the skills to continue building your own projects independently.

## Time allocation

- **Total lessons**: 6 lessons
- **Lesson duration**: 15 minutes each (10 minutes for Lesson 6)
- **Total course time**: 85 minutes
- **Project creation time**: 30 minutes per app
