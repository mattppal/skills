## YOUR 3 MAJOR HIKES (All Top-Rated Scenic Trails)

**1. Sunday: Waihee Ridge Trail**
- 4.2 miles, 2.5-3 hours
- Difficulty: Moderate-Hard (1,500 ft elevation gain)
- TOP 5 scenic hike on Maui
- Panoramic valley + ocean views
- 6am start, done by 10am

**2. Monday: Pipiwai Trail**
- 3.4 miles, 2.5 hours
- Difficulty: Moderate
- #1 rated hike on Maui
- Bamboo forest + 400-foot waterfall
- Part of Road to Hana day

**3. Wednesday: Haleakala Sliding Sands**
- **You're hiking:** 2-4 miles total (1-2 miles IN, then turn around and come back OUT)
- **NOT the full 18-mile trail** (that goes to backcountry cabins, serious backpackers only)
- Difficulty: Moderate (high elevation)
- Volcanic crater at 10,000 ft
- You have ~2 hours before sunset, so realistic distance is 2-4 miles max
