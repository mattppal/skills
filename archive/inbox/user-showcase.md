### Your Watchlists

[Your Watchlists](https://yourwatchlists.com/) offers a meticulously ranked list of the greatest films ever made, combining insights from seven respected "Top Movies" lists. Log in to mark movies as "Seen" or "Want to See," create your personalized watchlist, and share it with friends to compare your cinematic journeys.

From the creator, Brendan Baker ([@bpbaker20](https://replit.com/@bpbaker20)): "As a huge movie fan myself, I have always kept a detailed list of classic movies I felt I still needed to see in this lifetime... My sincere hope is that people use this site to expose themselves to movies they may not have otherwise watched, but with a little nudge (and perhaps some cinema-based FOMO), they end up experiencing some life-changing films as I have."

Thanks Brendan! ([@bpbaker20](https://replit.com/@bpbaker20), [X Profile](https://x.com/therealbrendanb))

### GPToggle

[GPToggle](https://gptoggle.com/) is an aggregator and intelligent selector of AI Models based on your prompt type. It empowers users to compare LLMs side-by-side, helping to test, toggle, and fine-tune AI responses for various tasks without guesswork.

From the creator, Lano Williams ([@onalius](https://replit.com/@onalius)): "GPToggle empowers users to make smarter, faster decisions about which AI model best suits their needs. By allowing side-by-side comparisons of multiple LLMs, it unlocks the ability to test, toggle, and fine-tune AI responses for tasks like writing, coding, or ideation—all without guesswork. In a world of ever-expanding AI models, GPToggle helps users cut through the noise and find the right tool for the job. Built entirely on Replit, it showcases how fast, flexible, and powerful solo development can be."

Thanks Lano! ([@onalius](https://replit.com/@onalius), [X Profile](https://x.com/onalius), [GPToggle X Profile](https://x.com/gptoggle), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lanowilliams/))

### Wayfinder Calculator

The [Wayfinder Calculator](https://wayfinder-calculatorv3.replit.app/) is an interactive maritime tool that shows shipping companies how much money and CO₂ they can save using Wayfinder's routing technology. It includes real-time fuel prices and dynamic visualizations to make complex efficiency calculations accessible.

From the creator, Shane Swiderek ([@sswiderek](https://replit.com/@sswiderek)): "This app is a game-changer for shipping companies! It instantly translates boring efficiency data into real dollars saved and emissions cut. Ship operators can literally see how many cars worth of CO₂ they're taking off the road, share results with stakeholders in one click, and make rock-solid business cases for sustainability tech."

Thanks Shane! ([@sswiderek](https://replit.com/@sswiderek))

### OnTapSEO

[OnTapSEO](https://ontapseo.com/) is an end-to-end SEO auto-pilot tool. It gathers information about a user's site, brainstorms targeted keywords, fills a content calendar, writes SEO-optimized articles with unique thumbnails daily, and publishes directly to connected websites like Replit or Webflow blogs.

From the creator, Gerrard Lipscombe ([@GerrardLipscomb](https://replit.com/@GerrardLipscomb)): "I run several online businesses, and SEO is the primary marketing channel. ### TagYourRide

[TagYourRide](https://tagyourride.com/) is a fun vanity license plate name generator. Users enter keywords, select their state, and the app generates six creative ideas that meet the state's character requirements, even rendering them on an image of the state's license plate.

From the creator, Brian Sallee ([@salleebrian](https://replit.com/@salleebrian)): "I was looking for an app to generate custom license plate ideas. I couldn't find one so I used chatgpt. Then I realized that this should exist as a standalone app, so I created it in less than an hour. Replit is awesome!"

Thanks Brian! ([@salleebrian](https://replit.com/@salleebrian), [X Profile](https://x.com/BSbriansallee), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/salleebrian/))
